Frequently Asked Questions

Can I wear my glasses with Magic Leap1?

You can't wear glasses with Magic Leap 1, but thanks to the Prescription Insert, you don't have to. It slots magnetically into Magic Leap 1 to give you the best possible viewing experience. Learn more.

How do I know which size Lightwear to buy?

Finding the right Lightwear size is based on IPD (interpupillary distance), which sounds tricky but it's actually very simple to calculate. If you wear glasses, your optician can tell you. Or download the Magic Leap Mobile App to discover yours. Still have questions?

Will I get a warranty with my Magic Leap1?

Absolutely. Every Magic Leap 1 comes with a 1 year hardware repair coverage provided by our 1-Year Limited Warranty Policy.
